# AI Tools I Use as a Technical Presales Consultant

## AI Tools I Use as a Technical Presales Consultant

AI is changing how individual professionals research, build tools, analyze data, and experiment with new technologies.

This site documents how I’m exploring AI in practice — the tools I use, the experiments I run, and the lessons I learn along the way.

As a Technical Presales Consultant, learning new technology quickly is part of my day-to-day work. Recently I started using AI tools to accelerate that process and to build small utilities that solve real problems.

This space is a public notebook of that journey.

***

### Why This Exists

AI tooling is evolving incredibly fast.

Most content online focuses on hype, demos, or tutorials that don’t translate well into real work.

I wanted a place to document:

• what tools actually help\
• how different technologies fit together\
• experiments that succeed\
• experiments that fail

Over time this will become a practical record of what it looks like to explore AI as an individual professional.

***
